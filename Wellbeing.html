How to relieve stress and anxiety from joining a new school

This webpage is designed to help you relieve any stress or worry that builds up because of you being worried of joining a completely new community, with people you've never met and a place that you aren't familiar with. But don't worry, this webpage will help you have a peaceful and confident mindset when you're on your first days of this wonderful school. Scroll down to start or use the button above!

Fear of not knowing people and difficulty making friends

One of the first things that you might me worried when joining a new school is about not knowing people and being scared to talk to people and becoming friends with them. This is very common as you are introduced to types of people that you know nothing about, especially in an unfamiliar area.

But don't worry! None of the students know anything about you either and there might be some new people aswell, so don't be afraid to talk to them. Try asking them about their hobbies, interests and other things which could help make you friends. But remember to be nice and tolerant towards others, as their norms can be different to yours. Also, it could take a few days to find someone who would want to be a friend, so don't worry about making a bad impression. And remember, you will always fit in.

Fear of not knowing teachers and school staff

One of the other things that students are worried about when joining a new school is the teachers and the school staff. You might feel nervous and anxious when talking to them because you haven't met them before and you could feel like you're bothering them. But don't worry! They are happy to help you with anything and they will try their best to help, so don't hesitate or be nervous around them.
