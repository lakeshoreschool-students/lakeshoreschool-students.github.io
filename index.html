Welcome to Lakeshore School Student!

Hello there! This website is specifically designed for new or old students of Lakeshore School. This is created to aid you in becoming familiar with the school or reducing stress/anxiety that comes from joining a new school. Click on the "How to reduce anxiety button" on the top of the page in order to access information that will help you reduce stress and anxiety when joining a new school, helping you increase confidence and help you meet people. Click on the "Information about your school button" in order to introduce yourself to the school without having to go to the school itself, so you minimise the risk of being in contact with COVID-19. Click on the school logo on the top right to go to the homepage. This button is avalible on every webpage.
